Privacy Policy & Support

Effective Date: July 2026

This page outlines the privacy practices and support channels for all mobile applications published by Constantin Horoshun on the Apple App Store.

1. Privacy Policy (Your Data is Safe)

Our baseline philosophy is simple: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

  • Local Computation: All processing operations (calculations, session notes, score trackers) occur entirely on your iOS device.
  • No Tracking: We do not deploy third-party analytics trackers, background monitoring scripts, or cloud data syncing structures.
  • No Ads: Our utility apps do not display intrusive ad networks that track your device identifier.

2. User Support Channels

If you encounter bugs, calculations errors, layout glitches, or have feature suggestions, please reach out directly via email:

📬 Email Support: daily.planner.developer@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all technical dispatches within 48 hours.
