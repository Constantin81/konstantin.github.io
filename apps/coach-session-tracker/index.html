Frequently Asked Questions

Does the tracker work without an active internet connection?

Yes. Coach Session Tracker functions entirely offline. All data queries, updates, and profile adjustments execute locally on your physical iOS device.

Can I use this app to log hours for certification?

Absolutely. The built-in counter displays aggregated practice history periods making it easy to cross-reference logs when submitting credentials validation paperwork.

Where is my confidential data saved?

All client metrics, financial tracking data, and notes remain encrypted directly within your iPhone storage file architecture, giving you total data ownership.
