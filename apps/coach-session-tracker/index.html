Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work without an active internet connection?

Yes. Coach Session Tracker processes your logs completely offline. You can review profiles and log notes safely even while traveling or working in areas with poor cellular coverage.

Can I use this app to track hours for ICF certification?

Absolutely. The integrated hourly counter and session logger provide an organized overview of your completed coaching history, making it significantly easier to compile your logs for accreditation.

Where is my client data stored?

Your privacy is a priority. All sensitive records, client notes, and logs are stored securely on your local iOS device, keeping data confidential and fully under your control.
